Decoding the Eternal Way of Living

Is Sanatan Dharma merely a collection of archaic rituals, or is it the most sophisticated, scientifically grounded blueprint for human existence ever conceived?

In the Sanatan series, author Anuj Rawal bridges the seemingly impossible gap between the logic-driven world of modern science and the profound, timeless wisdom of the Vedic Rishis.

The Biology of Belief

Discover how ancient practices trigger cellular renewal (Autophagy) and align our circadian rhythms with the cosmos.

The Psychology of Rituals

Explore how the 16 Samskaras act as vital neurological anchor points guiding human psychology.

The Physics of Space

Unveil the quantum reality of Vastu Shastra. Far from mere superstition, it is the sacred geometry of aligning our physical living spaces.

The Science of Dark Energies

Demystify the taboo realms of the occult and 'black magic' through the actual physics of frequency and energy transfer.
